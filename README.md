# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 26, 2026**

This privacy policy governs your use of the mobile application **PocketSax** (the "Application") created by **Alexander Kwan**.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
The Application does not actively collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from its users. 

*   **Personal Data:** We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical locations.
*   **Device Permissions:** The Application may request access to certain native features (like your device's camera or local storage) solely to execute core app features on your device. This data is processed entirely locally and never leaves your phone.

### 2. Third-Party Services
While the core app does not collect data, we utilize third-party development tools that may gather basic, non-identifying telemetry or system information to function. These include:
*   **Google Play Services / Apple App Store:** For app distribution, crash reporting, and generic, aggregated installation analytics.

### 3. Data Retention and Deletion
Because we do not collect or store any user data in external cloud databases, we retain no personal data. If you delete the Application from your device, all local app settings and data are permanently removed.

### 4. Children’s Privacy
The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent and discover your child has provided information, please contact us so we can take immediate action.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
